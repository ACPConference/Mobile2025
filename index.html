September 11-12, Renaissance Mobile Riverview Plaza Hotel, Mobile, AL

About The Event

Join us in Mobile, Alabama for the 2025 ACP Conference & Trade Show — a special celebration marking ACP’s 75th Anniversary! Set against the vibrant backdrop of America’s original Mardi Gras city, this year’s event promises dynamic educational sessions, expanded keynotes with workshops, TLI sales classes, and top-tier networking. Highlights include the Leadership Certificate Program led by University of South Alabama professors and a festive Mardi Gras-themed evening complete with a parade, cocktails, and dancing. Don’t miss this milestone moment — let the good times roll!

“Communicating at the Top of Your Game”

If you have ever experienced business communication problems, this interactive session is for you. In this session, we will explore some of the problems in business communication and suggest practical tools to deal with them. As publishers and managers, you are skilled in many aspects of communication. However, you may find yourself grappling with changing communication methods and others’ expectations. Moreover, your academic training and work experience may not have fully prepared you for what you are facing in business settings, such as negotiation, working in teams, hiring, firing, and managing employees and freelancers.

Renaissance Mobile Riverview Plaza Hotel

This hotel features an outdoor pool and rooms with flat-screen TVs and free Wi-Fi. All rooms at this Mobile Renaissance come with an iPod docking station and pay-per-view movies are available. A desk and refrigerator are also provided. Renaissance Mobile Riverview Plaza Hotel includes a gym, hot tub, and on-site restaurant serving seafood. Concierge and room services are available. The USS Alabama is 5 minutes from the hotel. Mobile Downtown Airport is 10 minutes away.
