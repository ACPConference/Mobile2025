About Liza & Jay Graves

Liza and Jay Graves are the team behind StyleBlueprint, a leading digital media platform celebrating Southern culture, travel, and local business. They bring together editorial insight and data strategy to help under-resourced towns strengthen tourism through co-op marketing that's both scalable and effective.

Liza Graves

Founder & CEO, StyleBlueprint

Liza started StyleBlueprint in 2009 to highlight Southern voices, small businesses, and meaningful travel. Her leadership has shaped it into a certified Woman-Owned Business with national reach and recognition for both its editorial impact and workplace culture.

She's been named one of Southern Living's Most Stylish Southerners and one of the Nashville Business Journal's Most Admired CEOs.

Jay Graves

COO, StyleBlueprint & President of EO Nashville

Jay brings over two decades of experience in tech, startups, and business development, working with clients like the NFL, NBC Sports, and major universities.

After co-founding and leading two companies through successful acquisitions, he now focuses on expanding StyleBlueprint's reach and refining its data-informed marketing tools for local partners.

Their Mission

Together, Liza and Jay believe that every Southern town has a story worth telling. With over three million readers each year, their work continues to uplift the people and places that define the American South.

Your Invitation to the First-Party Party: Digital Publishing And Email

Keynote Session - Day 3

For publishers, first-party data - especially email - is the most powerful tool you own. In this session, StyleBlueprint shares how they've built and leveraged a robust email strategy to grow audience engagement, strengthen advertiser relationships, and amplify content performance across multiple channels.

Learn how to:
  • Mix editorial and marketing content effectively
  • Streamline production using tools like RSS
  • Use email as the key to unlock retargeting, social, SEO, and more
  • Apply practical strategies tailored to community publishers

Through real-world case studies and actionable metrics, you'll walk away with practical strategies tailored to the unique needs and opportunities of community publishers in today's data-driven digital landscape.
