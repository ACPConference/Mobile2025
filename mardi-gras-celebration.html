Thursday Evening Mardi Gras Party Networking Dinner

Thursday Evening Mardi Gras Party Networking Dinner

Date:

Thursday, September 11, 2025

Time:

6:30 PM – 9:30 PM

Cost:

$109 per person

On Thursday, we take our Mardi Gras Celebration to the next level! Meet us in the hotel lobby to grab a drink and line up for our parade, complete with a second line band to lead us to the History Museum of Mobile for an evening of dinner and cocktails and dancing!

Help our Krewe crown the King and Queen of the soirée! Unwind and let loose at this one-of-a-kind networking experience you won't find anywhere else!

This event can be added to your registration for $109 per person. All food and beverages (including alcohol) are included in the price.
