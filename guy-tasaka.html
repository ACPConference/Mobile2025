About Guy Tasaka

Guy Tasaka is a digital publishing pioneer reshaping media for the digital age. As CEO of Tasaka Digital, he crafts innovative strategies for content distribution, focusing on mobile, digital subscriptions, and Connected TV.

Guy's insights on emerging tech regularly appear in Editor and Publisher Magazine. His leadership at Calkins Media and The New York Times has helped traditional outlets thrive online. Named 2015's Local Media Association Innovator of the Year, Guy continues to shape the future of local media with his forward-thinking approach.

A highlight of Guy's career is his "Local Media 3.0" playbook, a comprehensive guide helping publishers navigate and succeed in the digital landscape.

These days, Guy splits his time between Portland, Oregon and Honolulu, Hawaii. He's currently developing the next generation of media companies, leveraging cutting-edge data platforms and AI agents to revolutionize the industry.

With his expertise in analytics, strategy, and product development, Guy remains at the forefront of media innovation, always excited to explore new frontiers in the dynamic digital world.
