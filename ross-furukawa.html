Ross Furukawa

Media Entrepreneur & Digital Marketing Strategist

About Ross Furukawa

Ross Furukawa is a seasoned media entrepreneur and digital marketing strategist with a proven track record of innovating across both traditional and digital publishing environments.

Leveraging multi-channel lead generation tactics, AI-driven marketing automation, and integrated tech solutions, he has successfully launched and scaled multiple ventures—from local news platforms and design agencies to comprehensive affiliate networks—delivering measurable ROI and sustainable growth for brands and publications.

Lead Generation Tactics & First-Party Data: Boosting Local Media Sales

Discover how community publishers are driving revenue with smart, actionable strategies.

Learn proven frameworks for using contests, newsletters, and local directories to capture and monetize first-party data. The session will highlight real-world case studies from successful local media outlets and showcase affordable tech tools that make these tactics accessible to teams of any size.

You'll also get step-by-step guidance on audience segmentation and turning engagement into sustainable sales. Whether you're looking to grow leads, improve targeting, or boost ad revenue, this session will give you the practical playbook to get started.

The keynote session will cover:
  • Actionable frameworks for contests, newsletters, and local directories
  • Low-cost tech tools and platforms
  • Case studies of successful community publishers
  • Step-by-step guidance on audience segmentation and monetization

The keynote will be followed by a Q&A workshop with publishers to examine current tactics and advise on simple and actionable goals to implement.

Participants will leave with a follow-up playbook containing templates, checklists, and additional resources to implement these strategies right away.

Interactive Q&A Workshop

Apply lead generation strategies to your own publication!

Following the keynote, join this interactive Q&A workshop designed to help you apply the lead generation strategies to your own publication. You'll engage directly with fellow publishers to discuss current tactics, troubleshoot challenges, and identify simple, actionable goals tailored to your audience and resources.

Participants will leave with a practical follow-up playbook—complete with templates, checklists, and tools—to start implementing these strategies immediately and effectively.

Workshop Features:
  • Direct engagement with fellow publishers
  • Discussion of current tactics and challenges
  • Identification of actionable goals for your publication
  • Practical follow-up playbook with templates and checklists
